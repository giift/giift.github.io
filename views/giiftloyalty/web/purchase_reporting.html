Purchase Reporting

There are two ways to review or download all the transactions you are making through the API.

  • Connect to Giift.com and access the "Purchase Orders" Report directly from the reporting module.
  • Use the reporting API to access the data programmatically.

In both cases you will need a Giift.com access. Please send us an email, and we will be in contact to set up an account.

You will have access to all the transactions and their current delivery status.
